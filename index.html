Ethics over Everything

At Lush, we are not just amazing bath products, there is always a message behind our company and why we're here today. We choose people over profit, care over cruelty and ethics over everything. We advocate for sustainable plastic free packaging and 100% natural derived ingredients from all over the world. Every year, millions of animals including cats, dogs, monkeys, rats and bunnies are used for cruel research practices. The cosmetic industry is a billion dollar industry that often goes the cheaper route hiding these atrocities from the public. Not at Lush. Our goal is to educate others on why it is important to choose brands that care about everyone and everything. We put the love we have for all human beings and animals into our products and on our packages to educate others on this topic. Animal testing is not science, animal testing is not a cheaper way out and animal testing is absolutely unnecessary. For 27 years we have fought this fight and we will continue to educate and expand our brand with this message until all animals in research labs are free. Choosing ethical brands encourages companies to prioritize transparency, sustainability, and compassionate innovation.

Cruelty-Free Beauty

Since 1999, our team has been advocating to end these abusive cosmetic procedures on animals. We showcase our message through our packaging, campaigns and social media. Every year, over 20 million animals are test subjects in animal testing facilities. Most brands we use today cover these acts to hide what is really going on. Not at Lush, we encourage our consumers everyday that you can buy products you love without funding these research labs. We believe products for humans should be used on humans. Our goal is to educate as many people as possible to shop cruelty-free beauty products, hygiene products and so much more. You can put a stop to this. Supporting cruelty-free brands helps push the beauty industry toward more compassionate and responsible innovation.

Sustainable Ingredients

At Lush, we are not just animal rights advocates, we are earth advocates. Every year billions of pounds of plastic are dumped into the ocean contaminating our water and harming ecosystems. Our packaging is made using 100% recycled plastic and paper bags for our bath bombs and bubble bars. We also have a system set where if you bring us 5 finished bottles or any of our empty product containers, we will give you 1 product free. Promoting this idea gives customers an idea of how the smallest piece of plastic can make a huge difference for our environment. These naked products help minimize plastic waste and promote more sustainable cosmetic production.

Handmade Cosmetic Craftsmanship

Lush products are handmade in small batches using fresh ingredients. This allows greater quality control and reinforces the brand’s commitment to ethical sourcing. For example, our massage bar you see here uses real quality ingredients including Organic Shea Butter, Vanilla Bean, Fair Trade Cocoa Butter and cinnamon. Handmade production also supports transparency and reduces the need for large-scale industrial manufacturing.
